Match 'em

The Game

Match 'em is a matching game for young learners. A target picture is shown at the top of the screen — find the matching one among the other pictures!

Topics & Activities

Choose a topic from the top row (colours, shapes, pictures, and more), then pick an activity from the bottom row. Each activity uses a different background and set of pictures.

How to Play

In Touch/Mouse mode (the default):

  • Touch / click the matching picture on screen
  • Keyboard: press Space or Enter to advance
  • Gamepad: press any button to advance

Switch Access

Open the settings panel (the gear icon) to choose a switch scanning mode:

  • 1 Switch — a gold highlight automatically scans through the pictures. Press any key, button, or tap the screen to select the highlighted one.
  • 2 Switch — press Enter or button 2 to advance the highlight, then Space or button 1 to select.

The speed slider controls how fast the highlight moves in 1-switch mode.

Touch Controls in Switch Modes

  • 1 Switch: tap anywhere on the screen to select
  • 2 Switch: tap the left half of the screen to select, or the right half to advance

Tips

  • A correct match plays a fun reward animation with sounds
  • A wrong match plays a short error sound — keep trying!
  • Press the back arrow to return to the selection screen at any time
  • Your switch mode and speed settings are remembered between sessions
